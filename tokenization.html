What is RWA tokenization provider matching?

RWA tokenization provider matching identifies vendors that can legally and technically tokenize a real-world asset, including issuance, compliance rules, investor onboarding, custody, distributions, reporting and transfer controls.

Tokenization categories

Different assets require different legal wrappers, compliance standards and distribution models.

Real estate

Fractional ownership, SPV structuring, investor onboarding, rental income distribution and transfer limitations.

Funds and treasuries

Tokenized fund shares, money market exposure, reporting, redemption workflows and qualified investor access.

Private credit

Loan pools, collateral tracking, borrower reporting, institutional lender onboarding and yield distribution.

Securities

Permissioned tokens, transfer agents, cap table management, secondary trading and jurisdictional controls.

Commodities

Physical backing, proof of reserves, custody records, redemption rules and audit trails.

Multi-asset platforms

Flexible token standards and modular infrastructure for teams managing several RWA categories.
