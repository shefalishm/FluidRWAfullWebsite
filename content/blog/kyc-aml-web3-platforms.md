## Why Compliance Is A Workflow

Compliance is not a checkbox that happens once. Web3 platforms often need identity verification, sanctions screening, wallet risk checks, transaction monitoring, policy enforcement and recordkeeping.

The right compliance stack depends on what the platform does. A tokenized fund, a wallet, an exchange, a marketplace and a stablecoin payment product all have different risk profiles.

## KYC, KYB And Wallet Screening

KYC verifies individuals. KYB verifies businesses. Wallet screening evaluates blockchain addresses, counterparties and transaction history. Many digital asset workflows need all three.

For example, a tokenized asset issuer may need to verify investors, confirm eligibility, restrict transfers and monitor ongoing activity. A payment app may focus more on fraud, sanctions and transaction behavior.

## Questions To Ask Vendors

Before choosing a provider, ask which countries and documents are supported, how pass rates are measured, how manual review works, which sanctions lists are included and whether blockchain analytics are native or integrated.

Also confirm how data is stored, what reporting is available and how the provider handles escalations.
