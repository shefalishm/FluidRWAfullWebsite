What custody model is right for a tokenized asset project?

The right custody model depends on asset type, investor profile, regulation, transaction volume and operational control requirements. FluidRWA helps teams compare qualified custody, MPC wallets, embedded wallets, self-custody options and treasury operations providers.

Custody categories

Custody is not one decision. It affects onboarding, transactions, reporting and recovery.

Qualified custody

Regulated custody options for funds, institutions, advisers and security token workflows.

MPC wallets

Policy-controlled signing, approvals, key sharding, treasury management and role-based workflows.

Embedded wallets

User-friendly wallets for non-crypto investors who need simple onboarding and recovery.

Treasury operations

Wallet policies, permissions, reporting, settlements, payments and reconciliation.

Custody integrations

Connections into tokenization platforms, compliance tools, exchanges, payment rails and accounting.

Recovery and controls

Disaster recovery, approval workflows, transaction limits, audit logs and signer governance.
