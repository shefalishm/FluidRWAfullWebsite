Compliance FAQ

Short answers for tokenization teams evaluating providers.

Do tokenized assets always need KYC?

Many regulated RWA products need KYC, KYB or investor eligibility checks. The right approach depends on asset type, jurisdiction, investor type and distribution model.

Can compliance be enforced on-chain?

Yes, many token standards and platforms support permissioned transfers, wallet whitelists and rule-based restrictions. Off-chain checks are usually still needed for identity and documentation.
